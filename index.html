Ferrari 849 Testarossa

The Ferrari 849 Testarossa is a high-performance hybrid supercar that combines Ferrari's iconic heritage with advanced modern technology. Inspired by the legendary Testarossa of the 1980s, it features a sleek and aerodynamic design, a powerful 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, and three electric motors that together produce over 1,000 horsepower. The car delivers exceptional acceleration, reaching 100 km/h in about 2.3 seconds, while maintaining impressive handling and efficiency through its hybrid powertrain. With its luxurious interior, cutting-edge technology, and striking appearance, the Ferrari 849 Testarossa represents Ferrari's vision of the future while honoring one of its most famous models.

Ferrari 296 GTB

The Ferrari 296 GTB is a hybrid supercar powered by a 3.0L twin-turbo V6 combined with an electric motor, producing around 830 horsepower. It accelerates from 0–100 km/h in about 2.9 seconds and can reach over 330 km/h. Designed as a modern evolution of Ferrari’s performance philosophy, it blends extreme speed, sharp handling, and cutting-edge hybrid technology while still delivering the emotional driving experience Ferrari is known for. It features a compact and aerodynamic design that improves stability at high speeds, while the electric motor helps reduce turbo lag and provides instant torque for smoother acceleration. It can also run in pure electric mode for short distances, making it more efficient in city driving, and overall it represents Ferrari’s shift toward combining performance with modern hybrid efficiency.

LaFerrari

LaFerrari is Ferrari's first hybrid hypercar and one of the most iconic supercars ever built. Introduced in 2013, it combines a naturally aspirated 6.3-liter V12 engine with an electric motor to produce a total of 963 horsepower. Designed to showcase Ferrari's most advanced technology, LaFerrari features a lightweight carbon-fiber chassis, exceptional aerodynamics, and race-inspired engineering. The car can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in less than 3 seconds and reach a top speed of over 350 km/h. With only 499 coupés produced, LaFerrari is considered one of Ferrari's most exclusive and collectible vehicles.

Fonza SP1

The Ferrari Monza SP1 is a limited-edition speedster created as part of Ferrari's Icona series. Inspired by Ferrari's classic racing barchettas of the 1950s, the SP1 features a unique single-seat design with no windshield, giving it a pure and exhilarating driving experience. Powered by a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine producing 810 horsepower, the Monza SP1 combines breathtaking performance with elegant styling and advanced engineering. Its lightweight carbon-fiber construction and exclusive production make it one of Ferrari's most distinctive and collectible modern vehicles.
